JURST TRADE LTD

JURST TRADE LTD, is an established startup in the field of international trade. We as a team of professionals have decided to establish a startup for the sake of broadening our horizons and to carry out self-realization according our experience and know-how in the field of commodity trading. JURST TRADE LTD, is made up of four founding members with experience in areas necessary for the proper functioning of our trading house such as sourcing and outsourcing, purchasing, logistics, customs operations and of course sales. The principle of our work is based on ethical principles, transparency and profitability for our investors and the company.

More about us

Our Company

JURST TRADE LTD, is currently in a pre-seed fundraising phase where we are currently in the process of offering and attracting investors for our business. Our vision is to build an independently operating trade house within 4 years, operating on the European and American continents. As we are not a technology company and we are not interested in any speculation, we can evaluate the investors' deposits in a relatively short time with an interesting percentage coefficient and of course keep grow of the company. We are engaged in physical commodity trading, where the progress of the trade is clearly traceable and transparent for both our trading partners and investors. Our main strategical aim commodities are food ingredients, agrocultural commodities and metals.

In case, you are interested in possible cooperation in the field of investment in our company, we will be glad if you contact us and we will personally attend to you with deeper information and business model. We will explain to you the advantages and transparency of the investment, the growth potential as well as the interesting profitability of the startup.

Investments
